Our Story

Born from a
Mother's Love

Kit n Kiwi began in 2019 in a tiny Mumbai apartment with a sewing machine, a newborn, and a question every new parent asks: why is children's clothing either cheap and scratchy, or expensive and dull?

Our founder, Meera, believed children deserve clothing that is genuinely soft, beautifully designed, and honestly priced — clothes that keep up with the mess, the laughter, and the endless growing.

Organic Fabrics
GOTS-certified cotton, gentle on sensitive skin
Built to Last
Double-stitched, wash-after-wash durable
Joyful Design
Whimsical, age-appropriate, made to delight
Ethical Made
Fair wages, safe workshops, proud craftsmanship

Every piece we make carries the same intention I had on day one — that a child in Kit n Kiwi should feel completely, freely, joyfully themselves.

Meera Kapoor
Founder, Kit n Kiwi
